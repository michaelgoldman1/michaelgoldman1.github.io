Hi! Let me tell you a bit about myself...

I'm passionate about solving real problems for real people in innovative ways. For that reason, I pursue a combination of both software engineering and entrepreneurship (or intrapreneurship) in my career. Whether I'm the sole developer of an application or part of a large development team, I take ownership of the software I work on and I care a great deal about its quality in terms of user experience. User experience is not confined to the front-end but is all-encompassing. An app that takes too long to load data can quickly ruin the experience and lose customers. Similarly, poor security when exploited will ruin the experience for the user after the fact and will likely create distrust of the organization among existing and prospective customers. Thus, I keep the user experience in mind throughout all aspects of my work.

I have been fortunate to be the sole developer of a production application and have ownership of the product from design to performance to customer support. I loved the product management component of that role as well as the systems architecture aspect. I also enjoyed the wide variety of tasks which kept me on my toes. I seek opportunities which similarly utilize the breadth of my skillset beyond writing code.
